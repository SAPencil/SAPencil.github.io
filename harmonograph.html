What's a harmonograph?

This applet models a mechanical harmonograph, a mechanical apparatus that employs pendulums to create a geometric image. It can be viewed as a visualisation of harmonic consonance and dissonance, an area of particular interest to me as a musician. Reading Harmonograph: A Visual Guide to the Mathematics of Music by Anthony Ashton initially got me interested in harmonographs. Watch this video to get a quick idea of how they work.
