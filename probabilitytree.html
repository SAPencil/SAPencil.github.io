An interactive Probability tree

To help students visualise options

My students were really struggling to understand what a probability tree was for. They were trying to remember methods to use on them for the exam, but were failing because they really didn't understand the idea - the tree represents alternative realities, a path through the tree is one particular reality. I devised this visualisation to help them.
