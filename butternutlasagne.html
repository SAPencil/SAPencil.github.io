Goat's Cheese and Butternut Lasagne

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Goat's cheese 50 grams
Lasagne sheets 100grams
Chopped tomatoes 1 can
Milk 50ml

Step 1: Cook the tomato sauce.
Step 2: Roast the butternut squash in olive oil and garlic.
Step 3: Layer the lasagne sheets, tomato sauce, butternut squash and goats cheese.
Step 4: Bake for 45 mins.
