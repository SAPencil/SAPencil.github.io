A simulated Ant Colony

An exploration of random walks

This applet has a few 'walkers' who all have some amount of randomness in their directional choices, but tend to follow the leader. The leader ant has randomness in her directional choices too, but tends to follow YOU.

Try pressing arrow buttons to lure the leader up, down, left or right.
