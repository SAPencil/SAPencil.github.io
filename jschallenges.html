JavaScript mini challenges

edabit and interview cake

A key part of my learning of JavaScript has been through mini challenges like the ones on this page. They're not all that fun to play with, but they were fun to write! As well as thinking up my own, I get most of them from edabit and Interview Cake's weekly emails. For most, the bulk of the challenge came from controlling the logic. However, in each one I've tried to learn something new in JavaScript as well. And inevitably, in debugging while I go, I've had to learn and practice key concepts like hoisting, scope etc.
