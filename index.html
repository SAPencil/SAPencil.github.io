Who I am

from Teacher to Techie

I am a qualified Maths Teacher. And that’s what I’ve filled most of my day with for the last five or so years. It was fun! But at some point in that time, I caught the coding bug. And now it’s got to the point where I can’t ignore it anymore! I’m taking the plunge and getting myself into a tech job.

In my spare time I’ve been using online tutorials to learn JavaScript, HTML and CSS (along with some brief intros to other languages such as SQL). The original motivation was to find out how things work – I was troubled by the fact that I had no idea how 90% of the things I touched and interacted with worked. But once I found out how they did work, then I got really excited, and found myself engrossed in little projects, challenges and rabbit holes of inquiry. This website has a few such projects. I hope you enjoy them!
